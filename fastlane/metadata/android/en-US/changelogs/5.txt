Modified-Gompertz attenuation curve replaces the symmetric 4-parameter logistic. Same Bayesian-MAP machinery (LM solver, Gaussian attenuation prior, Laplace posterior, predictive band, ETA quantiles) — but the curve is now asymmetric, with a long flat top at OG, a sharp bend into descent, and a slow asymptotic approach to FG. The classic logistic could only approximate the lag plateau by warping its descent shape; Gompertz fits the lag region cleanly and reads out a meaningful lag time λ. Reference: Zwietering et al. 1990, Appl. Environ. Microbiol. 56:1875.

Other changes: a new "Conditioning" state replaces "Complete" — when SG drops below the iSpindle's noise floor the ferment is usually still biologically active (yeast cleaning up diacetyl, finishing maltotriose, CO2 desorbing). The label nudges the brewer toward the right next steps. Battery chart adds three coloured bands for the lithium-ion 18650 voltage zones (full / low / critical) with the y-axis locked to 3.0–4.2 V at 0.2 V tick steps. The model overlay stays visible during Conditioning and when the rate falls to zero, so users can read ferment shape post-hoc.

Internationalisation in 10 languages: zh, es, hi, ar, pt, bn, ru, ja, fr, de. Locale follows the system locale.
