Plateau detection on the SG chart. Lag plateaus, mid-ferment diauxic shifts (where yeast pauses between sugar populations), and asymptote tails are detected via a rolling-window slope test and shaded on the chart with an inline label. The estimate text now annotates "paused at SG X for Y h" when a mid-ferment stall is observed. The 4-parameter logistic fit itself smooths through the plateau — surfacing the detection separately is enough to read the diauxic shape off the chart without disturbing the v1 Bayesian FG/ETA pipeline. ROADMAP.md sketches a 7-parameter biphasic logistic and a ferment-segmentation pre-filter as future work.
