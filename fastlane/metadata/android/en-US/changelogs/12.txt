Mid-active pauses still shade through after the ferment moves on into later phases, so the diauxic shift stays visible even during cold crash.

The chart's model overlay now also extends through Stuck and Cold crash regions, drawing the historical Gompertz curve so the shape of the ferment is readable end-to-end.
