Calibration screen now has a sugar/water mixer at the top. Enter a vessel volume and a starting SG (e.g. 1 L at 1.080) and it tells you how many grams of table sugar and water to mix. A second box turns "I'm at 1.080, I want 1.060" into "remove 250 mL of solution, replace with 250 mL of water". Lets you calibrate four or five points across the range from a single starting batch instead of mixing each from scratch.
