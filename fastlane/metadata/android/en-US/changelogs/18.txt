The fermentation estimate no longer changes when you zoom the chart.

Switching the graph between 24h, 7d and All used to refit the model to just the readings on screen, so the original gravity, predicted final gravity and time-to-finish could all jump as you changed the window — a 24-hour view might even read a well-established brew as barely started. The estimate is now always fitted to the whole current ferment, picked out from your logging history automatically. The time window only zooms the chart; the numbers underneath stay put.

If you have more than one brew in the history, the estimate sticks to the live one instead of blending in a previous batch.
